Custom Domains for Landing Pages
What is a Custom Domain?

A custom domain allows you to use your own branded web address (like offers.yourbrand.com) for all your landing pages instead of our default domain.

Benefits of a Custom Domain
  • Consistent brand experience for your clients
  • More professional appearance in marketing materials
  • Better client recognition and trust
To set up your custom domain, visit the Account Info page where you can add your domain and request the necessary DNS configuration.
